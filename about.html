Our story

Designed from firsthand experience.

The idea for the Core Exercise Bar was conceived by Richard Sieben after many years in the construction industry, where he experienced recurring lower-back discomfort.

The product was developed around a non-exertion muscle-strengthening and stretching program intended to support movement of the back, neck, legs, ankles, wrists, hands, arms, shoulders and core.

Important: This website describes the product and its intended exercise uses. It does not provide medical advice. Consult a qualified health professional before beginning a new exercise program, especially when pain or injury is present.
